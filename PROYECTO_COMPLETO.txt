📞 INTERVIEW PREP

When asked: "Walk me through your project"

Start with:
  1. Problem: "Old forecast was linear, missed patterns (weekend vs weekday, seasonality)"
  2. Data: "16 months of daily sales, 350 product-store combinations"
  3. Approach: "Built baseline first (seasonal factors + day weights), then ML to beat it"
  4. Validation: "Temporal backtesting: trained on Apr, tested on May-Jun (no leakage)"
  5. Results: "48% better daily accuracy, 79% reduction in bias"
  6. Production: "Hybrid model takes monthly totals from baseline, daily split from ML"

Be ready for:
  • "Why baseline before ML?" → Baselines ground you, validate evaluation
  • "Why not ARIMA?" → 350 intermittent series, GB does cross-learning better
  • "How do you avoid overfitting?" → Temporal CV, backtest on unseen period
  • "What would you change?" → Monitor drift, add causal features, A/B test
